# [Lean](.md)

**Lean** - methodology used for elimination of waste in processes.

- Eliminates waste in workflows
- Focuses on value-added activities only
- Streamlines steps and reduces delays

- **Focus**: Streamlining workflows and removing non-value-added activities
- **Wastes eliminated**: Overproduction, delays, unnecessary movement
- **Ideal for**: Production, logistics, repetitive processes
